Domestic Violence

Domestic Violence is a very big thing in this society. It is a type of abuse by one person against another in a domestic setting. Domestic settings can relate to a home and/or family relations. Domestic Violence can fall under physical abuse, sexual abuse, economic abuse, emotional abuse, verbal abuse, isolation, and etc.
